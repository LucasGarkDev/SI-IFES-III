Gantt Chart Template V1.1

Author: Fernando Ribeiro <pinguim.ribeiro@gmail.com>

How To Use

INSERT LINES:

Insert the required number of lines: click the line number and drag down

From the template, copy the required section or just one line

INPUT DATA:

WBS: to itemize the WBS copy and past the required level from the template

Enter a Task and Resource Names

Enter a [Start] date and a [Duration] (working days); The Due date is calculated and the task bar shows the task's duration on calendar.

Or: Enter a [Start] and [Due] dates; The [Duration] (working days) is calculated and the task bar shows the task's duration on calendar.

Enter a percentage in the [% Complete box]; the task bar shows a progress bar to indicate what percentage of the task is complete.

Milestone: If you enter a start date without an end date, a milestone marker is displayed on the bar chart.

In Categories tasks (level 1) Start, Due, Duration and Percentage Complete values are calculated; however they can be overwritten.
